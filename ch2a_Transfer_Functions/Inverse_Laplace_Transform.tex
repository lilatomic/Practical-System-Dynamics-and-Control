\begin{section}{Definition}
	The mathematical definition of the $\ilaplace$ is
	\begin{equation}
		f(t) = \ilaplace[F(s)] = \frac{1}{2\pi j}\int_{c-j\infty}^{c+j\infty}[F(s)e^{st}]dt
	\end{equation}
	Hopefully you will never have to use it.
\end{section}
